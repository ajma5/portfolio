behind the lens

Amber Manahan

photographer + creative designer


Based in Raleigh, NC, I am a dedicated commercial photographer with a passion for creating compelling images. Specializing in photography for lifestyle and fashion brands, I also excel in capturing the essence of business locations. Whether I'm in the field capturing dynamic lifestyle product shots or meticulously composing images in the studio, my objective is to craft visuals that resonate and capture attention.


My background as a graphic designer complements my photography work, allowing me to understand aesthetics and branding deeply. This combination enriches my ability to deliver images that not only meet but exceed client expectations.


I thrive on the creative process, from conceptualization to execution, ensuring each project showcases the unique essence of the subject. If you're looking to elevate your brand through high-quality commercial photography, let's collaborate and bring your vision to life.
